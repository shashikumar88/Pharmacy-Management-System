Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about online consultations

What conditions can be treated through online consultation?

Our doctors can diagnose and treat a wide range of non-emergency conditions including cold/flu, allergies, skin conditions, chronic disease management, mental health, and more. For emergencies, please visit the nearest hospital.

How much does an online consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary by doctor and specialty, typically ranging from $25 to $75. This is often less than traditional in-person visits and may be covered by your insurance (check with your provider).

Can doctors prescribe medications online?

Yes, our licensed physicians can prescribe most medications when appropriate, except for controlled substances. Prescriptions can be sent directly to your preferred pharmacy or to MediCare for home delivery.

What technology do I need for a video consultation?

You'll need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and microphone, plus a stable internet connection. No special software is required - we'll send you a secure link to join the video call.

Is online consultation covered by insurance?

Many insurance plans now cover telehealth services. We accept most major insurance providers and can provide documentation for reimbursement if needed. Check with your insurance provider for specific coverage details.
