Product Description

Vitamin C 500mg Chewable Tablets from HealthPlus provide a convenient and tasty way to get your daily dose of this essential nutrient. Vitamin C, also known as ascorbic acid, is a water-soluble vitamin that is necessary for normal growth and development.

Vitamin C is required for the growth and repair of tissues in all parts of your body. It helps the body make collagen, an important protein used to make skin, cartilage, tendons, ligaments, and blood vessels. Vitamin C is needed for healing wounds, and for repairing and maintaining bones and teeth.

These chewable tablets have a delicious orange flavor and can be taken anywhere, anytime - no water needed. They are gluten-free and suitable for vegetarians.

Directions for Use

Adults and children 12 years and older: Chew one tablet daily or as directed by your healthcare professional.

Children under 12 years: Consult a doctor before use.

For best results, take with food. Do not exceed the recommended daily intake unless directed by a physician.

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.
