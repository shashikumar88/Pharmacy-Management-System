How To Choose The Right Device

Identify what health metrics are most important for you to track. Diabetics need glucose monitors, while those with hypertension should prioritize accurate blood pressure devices.

  • Chronic condition management
  • General wellness tracking
  • Post-surgery/recovery monitoring

Look for devices that are FDA-cleared or CE-marked, which means they've met certain standards for medical devices. This ensures better accuracy and reliability.

FDA 510(k) CE Mark Clinical Validation

Consider what technology features would be most useful for your situation:

  • Bluetooth/WiFi connectivity
  • App functionality
  • Multi-user support
  • Data export options
  • Battery life
  • Alerts and reminders
