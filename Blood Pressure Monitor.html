Product Description

The CarePlus Digital Blood Pressure Monitor is a clinically validated upper arm blood pressure monitor designed for home use. It provides accurate and reliable blood pressure and pulse rate measurements with its advanced measuring technology.

This monitor features a large LCD display with backlight for easy reading, automatic inflation with adjustable levels, and an irregular heartbeat detector. The cuff fits arms with circumferences of 22-32 cm (8.7-12.6 inches) and includes a cuff wrap guide to ensure proper placement.

The device stores up to 120 readings (60 per user) with date and time stamp, allowing you to track your blood pressure over time. It also includes a WHO blood pressure classification indicator to help you understand your readings.

Package includes the blood pressure monitor, standard cuff (22-32 cm), 4 AA batteries, instruction manual, and a convenient carrying case.

How To Use

Preparation
  1. Avoid eating, smoking, exercising, and consuming caffeine for 30 minutes before measurement
  2. Rest for 5 minutes before taking a measurement
  3. Sit in a chair with your feet flat on the floor
  4. Place your arm on a table so the cuff is at heart level
Measurement Steps
  1. Wrap the cuff around your upper arm (1-2 cm above elbow)
  2. Press the START button to begin inflation
  3. Remain still and don't talk during measurement
  4. View your results on the display when measurement is complete
For most accurate results, take measurements at the same time each day and record your readings.
