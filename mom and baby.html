Baby's First Year Milestones

0-3 Months

First smiles, head control, recognizing faces, cooing sounds.

4-6 Months

Rolling over, babbling, reaching for objects, first foods.

7-9 Months

Sitting without support, crawling, first words, pincer grasp.

10-12 Months

Pulling to stand, first steps, simple gestures, more words.
