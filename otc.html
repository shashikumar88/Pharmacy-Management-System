OTC Medicine Safety Tips

Important information for safe use of over-the-counter medications

Read Labels Carefully

Always read the Drug Facts label for active ingredients, uses, warnings, and directions. Don't exceed recommended dosages.

Avoid Medication Overload

Be cautious of taking multiple medications with the same active ingredients to prevent accidental overdose.

Consult Your Doctor

If symptoms persist or worsen after a few days of OTC treatment, consult a healthcare professional.
